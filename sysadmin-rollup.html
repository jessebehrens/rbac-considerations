SYSADMIN Rollup Considerations

Understanding when and why to roll up custom roles to SYSADMIN.

Why Roll Up to SYSADMIN?

The most important decision in your hierarchy: should custom roles be granted to SYSADMIN?

Advantages of Rolling Up

  • SYSADMIN can manage all objects in the account
  • Prevents orphaned objects when roles change
  • Enables centralized object administration
  • Follows Snowflake's recommended best practice
  • Simplifies disaster recovery and role rotation

Risks of NOT Rolling Up

  • Objects become inaccessible if role is dropped
  • Only role owner can modify owned objects
  • Creates hidden dependencies
  • Complicates auditing and governance
  • May block data migrations
